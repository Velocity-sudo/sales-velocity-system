# OBJECTIVE
Build a centralized, living Knowledge Base for a new client perfectly aligned with the Sales Velocity System. This document will be the single source of truth for all future strategies (Offers, VSLs, Ads, and GHL Automations).

# REQUIRED INPUTS FROM USER
When running this workflow, please provide or point me to:
1. Transcripts of Sales and Onboarding calls.
2. The Initial Onboarding Questionnaire answers.
3. Any other relevant files, links, or context.

# EXECUTION STEPS
1. **Deep Analysis**: I will thoroughly analyze the provided transcripts, questionnaires, and assets to understand the client's business, experience, and value.
2. **Create the Deliverable**: I will synthesize the information and create a new master file named `base_de_conocimiento_[nombre_del_cliente].md` in the current client's directory.
3. **Structured Formatting**: The file MUST be structured exactly as follows to serve our Sales Velocity framework:
   - `# BASE DE CONOCIMIENTO: [Nombre del Cliente]`
   - `## 1. Identidad y Autoridad` (Who they are, background, why people should listen to them).
   - `## 2. Objetivos del Negocio` (Current situation, desired revenue goals, ultimate mission).
   - `## 3. Avatar Premium` (Exact profile of the dream buyer, deepest pain points, desires, and objections).
   - `## 4. Oferta de Valor (Grand Slam Offer foundation)` (What do they sell? Promise, deliverables, pricing, and guarantees).
   - `## 5. Mecanismo Único` (What is their vehicle or unique method to get results?).
   - `## 6. Tono y Voz de la Marca` (How should we communicate in ads and VSLs?).
   - `## 7. Registro de Fuentes` (A log of which files/transcripts have been injected into this document).

# UPDATING RULE
This is a *living document*. If the user provides new call transcripts or information in the future while referencing this client, my priority will be to **update** this existing `base_de_conocimiento_...md` file, expanding its context, instead of creating a new separate file.
