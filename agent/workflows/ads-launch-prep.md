# OBJECTIVE
Ensure the integrity of a Meta Ads launch by running a strict checklist covering tracking, UTMs, creative structures, and alignment with the Sales Velocity System offers.

# REQUIRED INPUTS
1. The objective of the campaign (e.g., VSL views, Webinar Opt-ins, High-Ticket applications).
2. The exact URL of the landing page where traffic will be driven.
3. The client's Knowledge Base (`base_de_conocimiento.md`) and `$100M Offer` document.

# EXECUTION STEPS
1. **Audience & Offer Alignment**: Review the ad creatives/hooks and the Meta Ads targeting strategy. Cross-check against the Premium Avatar and Unique Mechanism defined in the `$100M Offer` document to ensure high resonance and click-through rates.
2. **URL Generation (UTMs)**: Generate the specific URLs with predefined UTM structures (e.g., `?utm_source=fb&utm_medium=paid&utm_campaign=[CampaignName]&utm_content=[AdSet]&utm_term=[AdId]`).
3. **Tracking & Pixel Validation**: Provide a checklist for verifying that the Meta Pixel, Conversions API (CAPI), and any GoHighLevel tracking codes are installed on the final destination URLs.
4. **Copy & Hook Audit**: Quickly review the ad copy. Does it adhere to the "Directness Directives" (no jargon, direct hooks, CTA drives to VSL)? Is the text optimized for the Meta feed constraints?
5. **Creative Folder Structure**: Outline the recommended folder structure for Media Buyers (e.g., organizing by Hook, Ad format, and Aspect Ratio).

# OUTPUT
A structured Markdown checklist (`ads_launch_checklist.md`) that acts as a final Go/No-Go report prior to activating the Meta Ads campaigns.
