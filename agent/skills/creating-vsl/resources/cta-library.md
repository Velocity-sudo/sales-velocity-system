# CTA Library - Optimized Call-to-Action Copy

The CTA is where conversion happens. Every word matters.

**Goal**: Reduce friction, eliminate anxiety, and make the next step crystal clear.

---

## **Best Practice: Use "My" Instead of "Your"**

**WHY**: Research shows +90% conversion increase when using first-person pronouns.

```
❌ BAD: "Start Your Free Trial"
✅ GOOD: "Start My Free Trial"

❌ BAD: "Get Your Discount"
✅ GOOD: "Get My Discount"
```

**Psychological Reason**: "My" creates ownership and commitment. "Your" feels like you're being sold to.

## **A/B Testing Strategy for CTAs**

Test these elements in order:

### **Test 1: Pronoun (Highest Impact)**
- Variant A: "Start Your Trial"
- Variant B: "Start My Trial"
(Expect: B wins by 50-90%)

### **Test 2: Benefit vs Action**
- Variant A: "Get Started Now"
- Variant B: "Transform My Business"
(Test which resonates more)

### **Test 3: Friction Level**
- Variant A: "Buy Now"
- Variant B: "See My Options"
(Lower friction often wins on cold traffic)

### **Test 4: Urgency Addition**
- Variant A: "Join Now"
- Variant B: "Join Now - Only 12 Spots Left"
(Test if scarcity increases or decreases conversion)

### **Test 5: Button Color**
- Green vs Orange vs Red
(Industry-dependent, test for your market)

## **CTA Placement Best Practices**

### **Frequency:**
- **Short VSLs (3-5 min)**: CTA at end only
- **Medium VSLs (8-12 min)**: CTA at 60% mark + end
- **Long VSLs/Webinars (20+ min)**: CTA every 5-7 minutes

### **Location on Page:**
- Primary CTA: Immediately below video
- Secondary CTA: After testimonials section
- Tertiary CTA: Bottom of page (for scrollers)

### **Button Size:**
- Mobile: Minimum 44x44 pixels (thumb-friendly)
- Desktop: Large enough to see without squinting
- Use white space around button (makes it pop)

## **Words to AVOID in CTAs**

These words trigger psychological resistance:

❌ **"Buy"** (use "Get", "Access", "Start")
❌ **"Submit"** (use "Send", "Continue", "Next")
❌ **"Purchase"** (use "Order", "Obtain", "Secure")
❌ **"Sign Up"** (use "Join", "Become", "Start")
❌ **"Download"** when selling (use "Access", "Get")
❌ **"Click Here"** (not specific enough)
❌ **"Learn More"** (vague, low conversion)

---

## **Power Words for CTAs**

Use these to increase urgency and desire:

✅ **Instant/Now/Today** (urgency)
✅ **My/Mine** (ownership)
✅ **Exclusive/Reserved** (scarcity)
✅ **Guaranteed** (risk reversal)
✅ **Free/Bonus** (added value)
✅ **Unlock/Access** (achievement)
✅ **Transform/Achieve** (outcome)
✅ **Limited/Last** (FOMO)

## **CTA Length Guidelines**

**Ideal Length**: 2-5 words

```
✅ Best (2-3 words):
"Start My Trial"
"Get Access Now"
"Join Today"

⚠️ Acceptable (4-5 words):
"Download My Free Guide"
"Reserve My Spot Now"

❌ Too Long (6+ words):
"Click Here To Start Your Free 30-Day Trial"
```

**Exception**: High-ticket CTAs can be longer if they clarify value:
```
✅ "Schedule My $10K Strategy Session"
✅ "Apply For 1-on-1 Coaching"
```

---

## **Multi-Step CTAs (for High-Commitment Offers)**

Break the commitment into smaller steps:

### **Step 1: Micro-Commitment**
```
"See If I Qualify" (instead of "Apply Now")
"Check Availability" (instead of "Book Now")
"Calculate My Savings" (instead of "Get Quote")
```

### **Step 2: Value Reinforce**
```
[After clicking, show value stack or testimonial]
```

### **Step 3: Final CTA**
```
"Yes, Reserve My Spot"
"Complete My Application"
```
