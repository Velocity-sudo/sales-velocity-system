# Creating Ads (Meta Ads 2026) - Strategic & Adaptive

This skill generates "Ultra-High Conversion" ad campaigns.
**CRITICAL GOAL**: Maximize conversion for the specific objective (Volume vs Quality).

## 🧠 Core Principles (Universal)
1.  **Terminology**: ALWAYS use "**Asistente**" (Never "Agente").
2.  **Visuals**: Use "Chaos vs Order" or "Old vs New" contrast.
3.  **Claims**: Must be grounded in the VSL/Offer reality (No "Magic Button" promises).

> [!IMPORTANT]
> ## 🎯 DIRECTNESS DIRECTIVES (MANDATORY)
> Every ad MUST follow these rules. No exceptions.
>
> ### 1. Hook = Avatar Identification (First 3 Seconds)
> The hook MUST name WHO the ad is for immediately. Not a question — a STATEMENT that makes the right person stop scrolling.
> - ✅ **CORRECT**: "La mayoría de las personas cometen el mismo error al comprar casa."
> - ✅ **CORRECT**: "Si estás buscando comprar casa en USA, necesitas ver esto."
> - ❌ **WRONG**: "¿Estás construyendo un legado o solo pagando renta?" (Too vague, could be anyone)
> - ❌ **WRONG**: "Si llamaste a un Realtor antes de hablar con un Mortgage Broker..." (Too specific, assumes action)
>
> **WHY**: Meta's Andromeda algorithm reads the text/audio of your ad to decide WHO to show it to. If the first line says "comprar casa", the algorithm finds homebuyers. Vague hooks = vague audiences.
>
> **AVATAR SCOPE**: Target ALL homebuyers, not just "dueños de negocio" or "self-employed". The ad speaks to ANYONE looking to buy a home. Specific niches (self-employed, ITIN holders) are mentioned as EXAMPLES inside the body, not as the hook filter.
>
> ### 2. No Jargon — Simple 3-Step System
> Replace ALL technical terms with a simple 3-step explanation anyone can understand:
> - ❌ "Protocolo Financing First" → ✅ "Un proceso simple de 3 pasos"
> - ❌ "Bank Statement Loans" → ✅ "Usamos tus estados de cuenta, no tus taxes"
> - ❌ "Non-QM programs" → ✅ "Programas especiales para ti"
> - ❌ "Underwritten Approval" → ✅ "Te digo exactamente cuánto te aprueban"
>
> **The 3-Step Framework** (use in EVERY ad body):
> 1. **Analizo tus datos** (estados de cuenta bancarios)
> 2. **Te digo exactamente cuánto te aprueban** (en 24 horas)
> 3. **Encontramos tu casa con aprobación en mano**
>
> ### 3. Algorithm-Friendly Language
> Include SEMANTIC KEYWORDS that Meta's algorithm uses to classify your audience:
> - "comprar casa", "hipoteca", "aprobación", "casa propia", "primera casa"
> - These words act as TARGETING SIGNALS — they replace interest-based targeting.
> - The more specific your language, the better Andromeda finds your ideal customer.
>
> ### 4. NO Video Directing — Keep it Simple
> Do NOT include video production directions (e.g., "Video estilo selfie", "Tono aspiracional", "Alta producción").
> The CLIENT records themselves with their phone. Keep it simple:
> - ❌ "(Video estilo selfie — Christian hablando directo a cámara, tono directo)"
> - ✅ Just write the SCRIPT. The client reads it and records.
>
> ### 5. CTA = Drive to VSL (MOST IMPORTANT PART)
> The #1 goal of every ad is to get the viewer to WATCH THE VSL. Not call. Not fill a form. WATCH THE VIDEO.
> Every CTA MUST reference the VSL video:
> - ✅ "Preparé un video de 14 minutos donde te explico paso a paso cómo puedes comprar tu casa con la mejor tasa. Da click abajo."
> - ✅ "Hice un video corto donde te muestro los 3 pasos exactos. Míralo aquí abajo."
> - ❌ "Agenda tu consulta" (Too aggressive, wrong funnel step)
> - ❌ "Descubre cuánto te aprueban" (Skips the VSL)
>
> **WHY**: The VSL does the selling. The ad just gets the click. If you send them to a form, they bounce. If you send them to a video that builds trust → they convert.

## 🔀 Step 1: Identify Strategy Mode

Before generating ads, determine the **Offer Type** from the Context.

### 🔵 Mode A: Webinar / Low-Mid Ticket (Volume)
*Use this for: Diego Páramo, Masterclasses, Challenges (<$500 public price).*
- **Goal**: Maximize **Registrations** (CPL < $5).
- **CTA**: "Regístrate gratis", "Separa tu cupo", "Clase Exclusiva".
- **Urgency**: "Cupos Limitados", "Se acaban los lugares".
- **Structure**: 3 Angles (Pain, Benefit, Fear).
- **Script Focus**: Broad appeal, curiosity, ease of use.

### 🔴 Mode B: High Ticket / Consultative (Quality)
*Use this for: Done-For-You Services, Consultative Services (>$2k).*
- **Goal**: Drive traffic to **VSL** → Qualified calls follow naturally.
- **CTA**: "Preparé un video donde te explico...", "Mira este video de X minutos", "Da click abajo para ver el video".
- **Urgency**: "Solo trabajo con X clientes al mes", "Estos datos cambian cada semana".
- **Structure**: 3 Angles (Error Común, Emocional, Urgencia/Datos).
- **Script Focus**: Simple language, broad avatar, drive to VSL video.

## 🛠️ Step 2: The Structure (Notion Template)

Regardless of mode, strictly follow the "**ADS QUE CONVIERTEN**" Notion structure:

### 1. ESTRATEGIA Y PRESUPUESTO
- **Budget**: Define based on Mode (Volume = spread; Quality = focused).
- **KPI**: Define the *One Metric That Matters* (e.g., CPL vs Costo Por Cita).

### 2. PLAN DE OPTIMIZACIÓN (Ramp-up)
- **Standard**: Test -> Filter -> Scale.
- **Cycle**: 4 Weeks.

### 3. ÁNGULOS Y COPY (Adaptive)

#### If Mode A (Webinar/Diego P.):
1.  **Angle 1 (Documentos)**: "Talk to Contracts".
2.  **Angle 2 (Excel)**: "Automate Analysis".
3.  **Angle 3 (Extinction)**: "Adapt or Die".
    *   *CTA*: "En el próximo webinar... separa tu cupo."

#### If Mode B (High Ticket):
1.  **Angle 1 (Error Común)**: "La mayoría cometen este error al [objective]. Yo hago lo opuesto."
2.  **Angle 2 (Emocional)**: "Si estás [pain state], esto es para ti. Hay una solución."
3.  **Angle 3 (Urgencia/Datos)**: "Cada mes que esperas, [cost]. Los datos dicen [fact]."
    *   *CTA*: "Preparé un video de [X] minutos donde te explico cómo [promise]. Da click abajo."

## 📝 Script Rules (The "V8" Standard)
- **NO VIDEO DIRECTING**: Do not write "(Video estilo selfie)", "(Alta producción)", etc. Just write the script.
- **Direct Address**: "Tú", never "Usted".
- **No Fake Promises**: Don't promise results without the mechanism.
- **Evergreen (Unless Specified)**: Avoid specific dates unless the user explicitly asks for a "Live Launch".
- **FIRST LINE = AVATAR** (Mandatory): The first sentence must identify the target person (broad: homebuyers).
- **SIMPLICITY > CLEVERNESS**: If a 5th grader can't understand it, rewrite it.
- **3-STEP MECHANISM**: Always explain the process in 3 simple steps. No fancy names.
- **SEMANTIC KEYWORDS**: "comprar casa", "hipoteca", "aprobación", "casa propia" for algorithm targeting.
- **CTA = VSL** (MOST IMPORTANT): Every ad ends with "Preparé un video de X minutos donde te explico cómo [promise]. Da click abajo."
