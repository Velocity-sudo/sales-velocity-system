**What is NotebookLM?**
- Google's AI-powered research assistant
- Allows uploading documents (PDFs, Google Docs, etc.)
- Generates summaries, Q&A, and audio podcasts from sources
- Excellent for synthesizing large amounts of information

**Known Constraints:**
- No official API (browser-based only)
- Requires Google account
- Limited to 50 sources per notebook
